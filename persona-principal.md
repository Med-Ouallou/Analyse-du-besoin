# Identifying the Main User

## Persona 1 – Cultural Tourist (Primary)

**Name:** Anas  
**Age:** 30 years old  
**Profile:** Passionate about art, history, and travel.  
He visits Morocco to discover the artisanal and cultural heritage of Fès, especially the medina, zellige workshops, and tanneries.  
He mainly uses his smartphone to browse cultural information and take photos, and his laptop to plan trips.

### Main Goal
To discover the craftsmanship of Fès while exploring how the city is embracing modernity and creative innovations.

### Key Needs
- Access articles and videos about traditional artisans and their techniques.  
- Discover how modern design, technology, and innovation are blending with Fès’s heritage.  
- Find reliable information on key places to visit (workshops, museums, galleries).  
- Easily save or share inspiring content on social media.

### Frustrations
- Scattered or outdated information about artisans and local initiatives.  
- Hard to find reliable sources that highlight both tradition and innovation.

### Environment
Uses an Android smartphone (for mobile browsing, photos, social media) and a laptop (for trip planning and long reads).  
Connects via hotel Wi-Fi and 4G while visiting sites.

## Persona 2 – Young Fassi Creator

**Age:** 23 years old  
**Profile:** Student in design, crafts, or digital arts. Passionate about local culture and new technologies.  
Seeks to reimagine Fès’s artisanal heritage using modern tools such as graphic design, 3D printing, or digital marketing.

### Main Goal
To promote Fès’s traditional heritage through modern creations and connect with other young creators and local artisans.

### Key Needs
- Access to resources about design, craftsmanship, and innovation.  
- Discover collaborative projects between artisans and contemporary creators.  
- Share personal creations and receive constructive feedback.  
- Find local opportunities (exhibitions, contests, workshops).

### Frustrations
- Lack of visibility for young local talents.  
- Few bridges between traditional artisans and modern creators.
